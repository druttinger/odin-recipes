Delicious Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 cup of pepperoni
  • 1 cup of green pepper
  • 1 cup of onion

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius
  2. Spread the tomato sauce on the pizza base
  3. Add the cheese, mushrooms, pepperoni, green pepper and onion
  4. Bake for 15 minutes
