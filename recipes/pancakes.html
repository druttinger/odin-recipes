Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, milk, egg, sugar, baking powder and salt in a bowl
  2. Heat a pan on medium heat
  3. Pour the batter into the pan
  4. Cook for 2 minutes on each side
